Our Philosophy

Empire Design specializes in custom home design throughout Metro Vancouver and the surrounding areas. Every project is a unique expression of how our clients truly live.

"A successful project is defined by how well design aligns with zoning, site conditions, and long-term goals. This clarity eliminates uncertainty."
Japnam Gill, Founder
01
Zoning Expertise

02
Site Analysis

Every design responds to orientation, topography, views, and context. We use this analysis to define feasibility and long-term value from day one.

03
Approval Strategy

How we
build your
home

A structured, transparent process from first conversation to the day you walk through your front door for the first time.

01
Discovery

We begin with an in-depth conversation about how you live, what matters, and what you have tried to find in existing homes and failed.

02
Design & Planning

Our architects produce full architectural drawings, structural engineering, and material specifications. Everything reviewed and approved before site work begins.

03
Construction

Our design team collaborates directly with your builder. Regular design reviews and detailed specifications ensure your vision is executed flawlessly. We're with you every step of the way.

04
Handover

A comprehensive walkthrough of every system, finish, and material. We provide a five-year structural warranty and remain available for any questions long after you move in.
